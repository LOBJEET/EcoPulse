## 📈 Launch Strategy

### Week 1: Soft Launch
- Launch to closed beta testers
- Gather feedback and fix bugs
- Optimize store listing based on user feedback

### Week 2-3: Regional Launch
- Target English-speaking countries first
- Focus on sustainability-minded regions
- Build community presence

### Week 4+: Global Expansion
- Expand to all countries
- Localize for major markets
- Ongoing marketing campaigns

## 📊 Success Metrics

Track these KPIs:
- Downloads (target: 1000+ in month 1)
- Daily Active Users (target: 30% of downloads)
- Rating (target: 4.5+ stars)
- Retention (target: 40% after 1 month)
- User reviews (respond to all feedback)
